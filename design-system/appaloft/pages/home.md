# Home Page Overrides

The generated master system recommends a social-proof landing pattern. Appaloft does not yet have
public customer proof in this repository, so the home page must not invent testimonials, ratings,
logos, or usage claims.

## Applied Direction

- Use a narrative landing page: product hook, platform primitives, deployment loop, domain model,
  positioning, interfaces, final docs CTA.
- Keep JetBrains Mono for headings and IBM Plex Sans for body copy.
- Use generated raster visuals for the hero and deployment loop.
- Prefer charcoal, white, emerald, cyan, amber, and coral accents. Avoid a page that reads as a
  dominant purple, beige, orange, or dark-blue theme.
- Keep hover effects to color, border, opacity, and tiny non-layout movement.
- Keep cards as repeated items only; do not wrap the primary experience in decorative card frames.
